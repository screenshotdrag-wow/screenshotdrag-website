🔒 Privacy Policy

1. Data Collection Purpose

We collect personal information for the following purposes:

  • Beta test participant management and Pro plan provision
  • Service improvement through feedback collection
  • Customer support and inquiry handling

2. Data Retention Period

Personal information is deleted immediately after the purpose is achieved. However, the following information is retained for the specified period:

  • Beta test participant data: 1 year after beta test ends (deleted on Nov 15, 2026)
  • Customer inquiries: 3 years (as required by law)

3. Data We Collect

  • Required: Email address, occupation, use case
  • Automatically collected: IP address, User Agent, Referrer URL

4. Third-Party Data Sharing

We do not share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • When you have given prior consent
  • When required by law or legal process

❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What are the main differences between the Free and Pro versions?

+

Key Differences:
• 📸 Capture Limit: Free 3 → Pro 100 (33x more!)
• 🏷️ Watermark: Free version has a watermark at the bottom
• 🎨 Editing Tools: Both versions include all editing tools

Is the Free version sufficient?

+

The Free version is sufficient when:
• Taking occasional screenshots
• Need editing occasionally
• Personal use
• Want to try the app features first

When should I consider the Pro version?

+

Pro version is needed when:
• Creating business presentations and reports
• Need continuous captures for bulk work
• Need clean images without watermark
• 3 captures are not enough

How do I purchase the Pro version?

+

You can purchase from Microsoft Store:
• Lifetime License: $49 (one-time purchase for lifetime use)

What are the system requirements?

+

Requirements:
• OS: Windows 10/11
• .NET 8.0 Runtime
• Memory: Low usage (~200MB)
• CPU: Low usage (~3%)

Can I use all editing tools in the Free version?

+

Yes! The Free version includes all editing features:
• Pen, brush, eraser tools
• Arrow, text, shape drawing
• Zoom in/out, pan (move)
• Undo/redo, save functionality
